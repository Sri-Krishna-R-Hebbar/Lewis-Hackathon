Project Description

A true masterpiece of a functionally dysfunctioning website, A Better Way to Define is a Website that builds websites. There will be three code editors: one for HTML, one for CSS, and one for JavaScript. The best part is that you can see your website come to life as soon as you fill them with your wonderful codes.

But... Here's a twist. Before you can run the code, you have to solve a word search whose link will be shared with you. This word search contains some HTML keywords hidden within it, which is both fun for new coders to get a grip on HTML keywords and enjoyable for professionals to solve before seeing their beautiful website. Once you solve the word search, send a screenshot to our website. This screenshot will be reviewed by Gemini. Once accepted, you’ll get your turn to run your website and showcase your creation.

Whether you’re a seasoned coder or a curious newbie, our platform offers a hands-on approach to learning. Experiment, create, and see your results in real-time—all while solving puzzles and enjoying the thrill of discovery.
